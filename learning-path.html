七步学习路径Seven-Step Learning Path

先读懂市场和产品风险，再进入训练和社群Understand markets and product risk before training and community learning.

每一步只回答一个核心问题，避免学习者一开始就被大量课程名称淹没。Each step answers one core question so learners are not overwhelmed by course names too early.

Step 1 金融市场基础Financial Market Basics

了解资产类别、市场参与者、价格形成和常见市场术语。Understand asset classes, market participants, price formation and common market language.

 Step 2 外汇与主要货币对Forex & Major Currency Pairs

学习汇率、点差、报价方式、主要货币对和宏观变量。Learn exchange rates, spreads, quote conventions, major pairs and macro drivers.

 Step 3 CFD 产品与杠杆CFD Products & Leverage

理解保证金、杠杆、强平、成本和产品风险边界。Understand margin, leverage, close-out, costs and product-risk boundaries.

 Step 4 风险管理Risk Management

建立仓位、止损、风险预算、情景分析和亏损承受框架。Build frameworks for sizing, stops, risk budgets, scenarios and loss tolerance.

 Step 5 交易心理Trading Psychology

训练纪律、耐心、复盘、情绪管理和交易计划执行。Train discipline, patience, review habits, emotional control and plan execution.

 Step 6 案例演练Guided Practice

通过案例复盘、交易日志模板和风险预算练习建立流程意识。Build process discipline through case reviews, journal templates and risk-budget exercises.

 Step 7 加入学习社群Join Learning Community

与其他学习者交流市场知识、活动信息和学习进度。Discuss market education, event updates and learning progress with other learners.
